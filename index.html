About Sultana's Brand

Inspired by my mother's expertise in sewing and embroidery, I began practicing various stitches back in 2017. In December of last year, I decided to learn embroidery properly and continued until mid-2025. During this time, I explored different stitches, the history of embroidery, various sectors within the field, and color combinations. Although I didn’t receive much positive feedback, I believe every effort deserves a place in my showcase. So, I created this website using HTML and CSS to represent my small journey. I also have a future business plan for Sultana’s Brand, as I dream of contributing to reducing unemployment in my country—there are so many skilled and talented people in this craft.
